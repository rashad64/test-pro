Computer user and programmer

Computer user are everyday life people who perform task, obtain information only such as lawyers, teachers, government people. Programmers on the other hand are the people behind the scenes who writes the program that regular people use

Servers

A server, is a computer used to host programs and data for a small network. typically larger, and more powerful, and more expensive than a desktop computer, a server is usually located in an out of the way place and can serve many users at one time. User connect to the server through a network, using their desltop computer , portable computer, thin client, or a dumb terminal consisting of just a monitor and keyboard.
